About me

I am an applied economist with experience translating empirical evidence into clear inputs for decision making.

I see economics as a practical tool for understanding how people, organisations, and systems respond to incentives and constraints. I am interested in using empirical analysis to clarify complex problems and inform real-world choices.

I have worked in academic and applied research environments in Europe and North America, I am currently an Economic Researcher at the Ellison Institute of Technology, Oxford.
